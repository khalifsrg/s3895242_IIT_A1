Ideal Job

AGL Cybersecurity Lead Advisor Role

Cyberscurity Lead Advisor

A cybersecurity advisor is in charge of acting as the key communicator between security teams and business units. They are the individuals that establish, build and maintain the working relationships with the internal and external stakeholders of the company, in this context it would be AGL. They are in charge of supervising and maintaining situation awareness of cyber security issues and to keep business leaders informed on the status of the problems and updates.

They maintain stability in the company through educating security communities of interest to raise awareness on different cyber security and IA issues as required. Along with this, they are also the first to reach for credible information and positive security advice. An advisor understands security technology, solutions, processes and procedures and is able to translate technical concepts into business concepts. In essence, a cybersecurity lead advisor manages the team of system architects, designers and business unit facing engineers.

Along with that, I also intend on working my self up to a high position such as an advisor as it is something I desire. I enjoy working hard, and it would genuinely be satisfying for me to be able to finish off my tasks one by one. On top of the previous points, I also intend to earn a higher wage than a regular employee.

What are the skills, qualifications and experience for the job?

To be accepted into the role the individual must have already been certified in information security and/or have experience with information security as part of an ICT role. They must also possess expert knowledge and experience in IT security and/or ICT policy, particularly computer security, network security and computer network defence. Knowledge of frameworks, processes and methodologies that contribute to excellent client engagement is required, while also maintaining highly developed communication and relationship management skills when interacting with clients and business leaders.

Cultural Background

My parents were born and raised in Indonesia and moved to Melbourne, Australia 4 years before I was born, which was the year of 2002. I was raised in Melbourne for all my life, however my parents continued to teach me Indonesian culture and language, allowing me to be able to speak fluent my parents mother tongue. I am extremely grateful to my parents to have taught me to speak a language other than English as it has allowed for me to create relationships with people that I would not have been able to meet without being able to speak it, I also hope to continue to learn to read write in many other languages in order to socialise with more people in the multicultural society that is Melbourne and beyond.
